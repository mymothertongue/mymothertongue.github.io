Justice System

This is the biggest joke. Due to a police investigation that I was unfortunately involved in (I did not do anything), the police asked to check my phone.

No problem, I handed it over. I have no idea what they did, but it just stopped working. The display completely failed.

So, I asked for a repair or replacement. Instead of that, I was told this is a common issue with my phone model, and to prove it, they sent me a YouTube video with a repair tutorial.

I'm still trying to get compensation, but the police department has been useless, and now they're asking me to contact the local council for the claim. Wish me luck!

This is another example of the lack of accountability that this country has, and at the same time they bother you with their silly democratic system.
